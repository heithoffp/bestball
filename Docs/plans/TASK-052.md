# TASK-052: Portfolio-wide pick quality summary — value/reach histogram by ADP round

**Status:** Draft
**Priority:** P3

---

## Objective

Add a portfolio-level view that shows the distribution of pick quality (value vs. reach vs. fair) across all rosters, broken down by ADP round band. The ADP Tracker shows individual player value but there is no aggregate "how am I doing at each round" view. This helps drafters understand where in the draft they consistently overpay or get value, without requiring them to manually review every pick.

## Dependencies

None

## Open Questions

- Should this live on the Dashboard as a new widget, or as a new view within the ADP Tracker tab?
- What thresholds define value vs. fair vs. reach (e.g., >3 picks early = value, <3 picks late = reach)?
