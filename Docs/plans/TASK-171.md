# TASK-171: Creator and streamer outreach — target list and pitch templates

**Status:** Draft
**Priority:** P1

---

## Objective
Identify best-ball content creators and streamers (YouTube, Twitter/X, podcasts, Twitch) who cover Underdog or DraftKings best-ball drafts and could benefit from the tool. Create a prioritized target list (10-20 creators) with platform, audience size, and contact method. Draft personalized pitch templates that offer free Pro access in exchange for trying the tool on-stream or mentioning it in content. Per developer feedback, streamers reject distracting overlays — the pitch should emphasize that the web app is the primary product (non-intrusive, use between drafts) and the overlay is optional and subtle.

## Dependencies
TASK-164 (messaging and value prop to inform pitch)

- Include a "creator program" landing page or just direct DMs?
