# TASK-201: Customer comms for Chrome review window

**Status:** Draft
**Priority:** P2

---

## Objective
Communicate the Chrome Web Store review status to paying customers and reduce support load while the 1.0.3 appeal is pending (estimated 2-4 weeks). Scope: (1) draft and send an email to existing paying customers explaining that 1.0.2 still works on underdogfantasy.com but the extension breaks for users migrated to underdogsports.com, and offering the sideload zip (TASK-200) as a stopgap; (2) add a banner or notice on the marketing site noting the pending review and that existing customers on the old Underdog domain are unaffected; (3) define a refund policy for the affected window (suggest no-questions-asked refunds for users who can't access the new domain). Goal is to retain trust and prevent public complaints during the rejection window. Removable once the store review completes.
