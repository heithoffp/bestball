## Overview

Reach out to best-ball streamers and content creators via direct DM. Offer permanent free Pro access in exchange for on-stream usage. No creator landing page — all outreach is personal and direct.

**Who we're targeting:** Micro-creators (even <1K followers) who specifically stream or create content about best-ball drafts on Underdog and/or DraftKings. Not general fantasy football channels — best-ball focused.

**What we're offering:** Permanent free Pro access. No strings, no scripts, no affiliate tracking. If they find it useful and mention it on stream, great. If not, they keep the access anyway.

**Tone:** Community member sharing a tool they built, not a brand doing influencer marketing. Match the Reddit post tone from TASK-170.

## DM Pitch Template

Keep it under 150 words. Lead with the problem, not the product. Personalize every message.

---

**Subject / Opening line:** Hey [CREATOR_NAME] — built something you might find useful for your drafts

**Body:**

Hey [CREATOR_NAME], I've been watching your [SPECIFIC_CONTENT — e.g., "live best ball drafts on YouTube" / "best ball strategy breakdowns" / "portfolio recaps"]. Good stuff.

I built a portfolio analytics tool for best-ball drafters — upload your rosters from [PLATFORM_THEY_USE] and see your full portfolio on one screen: exposures, archetypes, stacking patterns, draft tendencies across all your entries. Zero setup, just upload and explore.

It's at bestballexposures.com — there's sample data you can poke around with without uploading anything.

I'd love to give you permanent free Pro access if you want to try it out. No strings — just thought it'd be useful for someone drafting as many teams as you do. If you end up liking it and want to pull it up on stream sometime, that'd be awesome, but no pressure.

Let me know if you're interested and I'll set up your account.

---

**Personalization checklist (fill in before sending):**
- [ ] `[CREATOR_NAME]` — their name or handle
- [ ] `[SPECIFIC_CONTENT]` — reference something specific they've made (a video title, a take, a draft result)
- [ ] `[PLATFORM_THEY_USE]` — Underdog, DraftKings, or both
- [ ] Confirm the DM platform is one they're active on (don't DM on a dead account)

## Follow-Up Template

Send once, 5-7 days after the initial DM, only if no response. Do not follow up more than once.

---

Hey [CREATOR_NAME] — just bumping this in case it got buried. I built a best-ball portfolio tool (bestballexposures.com) and wanted to offer you free Pro access. No rush and no pressure — just wanted to make sure it landed. Happy to answer any questions if you're curious.

---

**Rules:**
- One follow-up maximum. If no response after the follow-up, move on.
- Never follow up on a platform they're not active on.
- If they respond with "not interested" or similar, thank them and stop.

## Deal Terms

These terms should be communicated casually in conversation, not as a formal agreement. No contracts, no legalese.

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **What they get** | Permanent free Pro access ($20/mo value) — no expiration, no clawback |
| **What we hope for** | They try the tool and, if they genuinely find it useful, use it on stream or mention it in content |
| **What we don't require** | No scripted mentions, no posting requirements, no affiliate tracking, no exclusivity |
| **If they stop using it** | They keep Pro access. No consequences, no awkward conversations |
| **Promo code (optional)** | If they want to offer their audience a discount, we can create a custom promo code (e.g., "JEARY" for 25% off). This is optional and only offered if they ask or seem enthusiastic |
| **How access is granted** | Developer manually flags their Supabase account as Pro tier after they sign up |

**The pitch is simple:** "Free tool, use it if you like it, mention it if you want to." Not "free tool in exchange for promotion." The difference matters — creators can smell transactional pitches.
