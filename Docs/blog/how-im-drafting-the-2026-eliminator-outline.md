# Overview
This purpose of this article is to introduce players into THe Eliminator contest and highlight the main differences between this and traditional BBM style tournaments. I want to present this in a non-traditional way, but guiding the reader through one of my Eliminator drafts and with each pick I show maybe 3 or 4 candidate players, the players I select, and explain my selection while interweaving the Eliminator special learnings associated with that pick.

With each pick I want a simple visual showing the pick I chose, other available picks, and how much "bye week rainbow" is filling out, similar to what the chrome extension shows

# Pick 2 - Justin Jefferson
This is a pairing you would rarely get in BBM, but is slightly more common in the eliminator. Justin Jefferson is a smash pick, but his downside is that he has to survive week 6. Talk about early bye weeks coming at a discount and list some players like Mahomes, Tetaroia mcmillan, etc. However, also talk about how week 6 byes are not as bad as people think because of the other high powered offenses that are also on bye that week including names like Jahmyr gibbs, Amon Ra, and Jamaar Chase.

# Pick 3 = Josh Jacobs
Talk about how we are aiming for a more balanced team, the 3/5/7/3 structure, and how teams drafting are more RB hungry and josh jacobs is the last RB before a tier drop so it was an obvious pick.

# Pick 4 - Chris Olave
Keeping the team balanced. Perhaps talk about Olave being a good pick because of the floor he brings and Eliminator is primarily about building a great floor rather than absolute ceiling.
