Best Ball Exposures — Portfolio Analytics for Underdog & DraftKings Best Ball

Best Ball Exposures (BBE) is a portfolio analytics SaaS for fantasy football best-ball drafters. Sync your Underdog Fantasy and DraftKings rosters and see exposure, roster archetypes, team stacks, draft flow, and ADP trends across your whole portfolio in one place.

Open the app · Get the Chrome extension

Features

  • Portfolio dashboard — exposure, archetypes, and portfolio shape on one screen.
  • Roster archetypes — every roster classified across RB, QB, and TE tiers (RB Hero, Zero RB, Hyper Fragile, Value).
  • Draft overlay — live exposure and combo analysis built into the Underdog and DraftKings draft screens.
  • Multi-platform support — unified view across Underdog Fantasy and DraftKings.
  • Combo & stacking analysis — surface QB-WR stacks and correlation plays across your entries.
  • ADP tracking — see who is rising and falling so you know where the value is.
  • Draft flow analysis & player rankings — Pro features for deeper portfolio shaping.

Pricing

  • Free — $0. Portfolio dashboard, exposure analysis, roster viewer with archetypes, multi-platform sync.
  • Pro — $20/month ($15 with a creator promo code). Adds draft flow analysis, combo & stacking, ADP tracker, player rankings, and roster construction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Best Ball Exposures?

A portfolio analytics tool for fantasy football best-ball drafters. It syncs Underdog and DraftKings rosters and shows exposure, archetypes, stacks, draft flow, and ADP trends across every entry.

Which platforms does it support?

Underdog Fantasy and DraftKings best-ball contests, unified in a single portfolio view.

Is it free?

Yes. The Free tier covers the dashboard, exposure analysis, roster viewer with archetypes, and multi-platform sync. Pro is $20/mo (or $15 with a creator promo code).

How does syncing work?

The Chrome extension auto-syncs Underdog and DraftKings rosters as you draft. Data is stored locally (IndexedDB) or in your account if you sign in.
