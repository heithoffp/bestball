What is Best Ball Exposures?

Best Ball Exposures is a portfolio analytics tool for fantasy football best-ball drafts. Sync your Underdog and DraftKings rosters and explore your whole portfolio in one place: player exposures, ADP movement, QB stacks and combos, custom rankings, and a live draft companion. It works on the web at bestballexposures.com and on iOS.

Frequently asked questions

How do I sync my drafts?

On desktop, install the Best Ball Exposures browser extension and open your Underdog or DraftKings draft pages. The extension reads your rosters and syncs them to your account. On mobile, use the desktop hand-off flow from the app to bring your synced portfolio to your phone.

Which platforms are supported?

Best Ball Exposures supports Underdog and DraftKings best-ball drafts.

Do I need an account?

You can explore the app in guest mode with local storage. Creating a free account enables cloud storage and sync across your devices.

How does the Pro subscription work?

Best Ball Exposures offers a free tier and a paid Pro tier. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. For full details, see our Terms of Service.

How do I cancel or request a refund?

Subscriptions purchased through the App Store are managed in your Apple ID subscription settings on your device. Refund requests for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. For anything else, email us at bestballexposures@gmail.com and we will help.

How is my data handled?

You retain ownership of the roster data you upload or sync, and we use it solely to provide the Service. We do not sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details, including the Best Ball Arena opt-out and roster takedown path.

How do I remove a roster from the Best Ball Arena?

You can remove your own teams from the Arena at any time using the account-level enrollment switch. If you recognize any roster and want it removed, email bestballexposures@gmail.com and we will remove it.
