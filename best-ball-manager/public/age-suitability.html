Age Suitability

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This page explains who Best Ball Exposures is for and how we think about age suitability. It goes along with our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Best Ball Exposures is for adults 18 and older (or the minimum legal age where you live, which can be higher in some states).

What Best Ball Exposures Is

Best Ball Exposures is an analytics tool for fantasy football best-ball drafting. It helps you understand the teams you draft: how exposed you are to each player across your portfolio, how your rosters are built, your stacks, where ADP is moving, and draft help while you go.

It only reads your draft data. It does not offer or run any kind of gambling, betting, or wagering. It does not run contests, and it never takes, holds, or pays out entry fees. The only money involved is an optional subscription that unlocks the analytics.

Why the Service Is for Adults

People use Best Ball Exposures alongside the best-ball drafts they run on other platforms, including Underdog and DraftKings. Those platforms only let adults take part, and the drafting itself is meant for an adult audience. Since our tool is here to help with that, we make it for adults too, and we ask that everyone using it be at least 18 (or older where local law requires). We are not affiliated with or endorsed by Underdog, DraftKings, or any fantasy platform.

About Our Content

There is no violent, sexual, or mature content anywhere in the app, and no casino-style games. We treat it as an adult product because of what it is about, a companion to adults-only fantasy football drafting, not because of anything shown inside it.

Eligibility

  • You need to be at least 18, or older if your state or country sets a higher age, to use the Service.
  • By using it, you confirm that you meet that age requirement.
  • If we find out that someone under the required age has made an account, we will remove it.
