Best Ball Exposures is the companion extension for bestballexposures.com — a portfolio analytics tool built for serious best-ball drafters.

If you're 50 entries deep and can't remember what you drafted last Tuesday, this is for you.

THE PROBLEM
Most tools show you one roster at a time. When you're managing a real portfolio across Underdog Fantasy and DraftKings, you need to see all of it — exposure, strategy patterns, and correlation plays across every entry.

WHAT THIS EXTENSION DOES
This extension connects your draft platforms to Best Ball Exposures. It does two things:

1. Syncs your completed rosters from Underdog Fantasy and DraftKings into your Best Ball Exposures account — no manual uploads, no copy-pasting.

2. Shows a live draft overlay during your best-ball snake drafts. While you're on the clock, you'll see your current portfolio exposure and correlation context for each player on the board — inline, without a second window or alt-tabbing.

WHAT YOU GET ON BESTBALLEXPOSURES.COM
Your synced data powers a full analytics dashboard:
• Portfolio Dashboard — exposure, archetypes, and portfolio shape on one screen
• Roster Archetypes — every roster classified by RB, QB, and TE strategy so you can see what you're actually building
• Combo & Stacking Analysis — find which QB-WR stacks and correlation plays keep showing up
• ADP Tracking — see who's rising and falling before your next draft
• Player Rankings — custom tier rankings that feed into the draft overlay
• Multi-platform support — Underdog and DraftKings rosters in one place

Free tier includes the dashboard, exposure analysis, roster viewer with archetypes, and multi-platform sync. Pro tier adds the draft overlay, combo analysis, ADP tracker, player rankings, and roster construction tools.
